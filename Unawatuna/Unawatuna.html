Unawatuna

Is Unawatuna worth visiting? Yes! It's the most popular beach in Sri Lanka and a great place to visit for a beach getaway. There are plenty of things to do in Unawatuna and also in the surrounding the area as well including Jungle Beach, Galle, Wijaya Beach and more! Unawatuna is a major tourist attraction in Sri Lanka and known for its beach and corals. It is a suburb of Galle, about 5 kilometres (3.1 mi) southeast to the city center and approximately 108 kilometres (67 mi) south of Colombo.
